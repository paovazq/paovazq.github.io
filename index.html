About Me

Hi everyone! I'm Norma, also known as Pao. I'm currently a third year student at Cal State Fullerton. GO TITANS! My blog is based around my experiences and different styles of photography. I carry my handy dandy, Canon 70D camera everywhere I go incase there's adventure in need of filming or if I ever casually run into Khalid. I've fallen in love with this hobby of mine and I hope I can make you fall in love with it through my eyes..or lens in this case.
